Welcome To Woodbine Modern School, Darbhanga

Education | Innovation | Leadership

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of excellence in education, inspiring a lifelong love of learning and fostering the growth of well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of an ever-changing world. We aim to create an inclusive and diverse community where every student's unique talents and abilities are recognized and celebrated.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive academic program that is designed to challenge and inspire our students. Our dedicated and experienced faculty members are committed to fostering a love of learning and providing the skills and knowledge necessary for success in the 21st century. We offer a wide range of courses, from the humanities and sciences to the arts and technology, ensuring that each student has the opportunity to explore their passions and interests.

Character Development

We believe that education goes beyond textbooks and test scores. Character development is a fundamental part of our educational philosophy. Through our values-based education, we instill qualities such as integrity, responsibility, empathy, and resilience in our students. We aim to mold them into compassionate and ethical individuals who can make a positive impact on their communities.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to our rigorous academic curriculum, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities that allow students to explore their interests, develop new talents, and build lasting friendships. From sports teams to art clubs, debate societies to community service projects, there is something for everyone.
