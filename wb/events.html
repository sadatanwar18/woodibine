Events

At Woodbine, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom, and our calendar of exciting school events is a testament to this philosophy. Our events are designed to enrich the educational experience, foster a sense of community, and create lasting memories for our students, parents, and the wider community.

Arts Extravaganza

Our students' artistic talents shine through displays of visual arts.

Felicitation Ceremony

We take immense pride in honoring and celebrating the outstanding achievements and contributions of our students.

Quiz Competition

Our annual Quiz Competition stands as a testament, offering an exciting platform for students to showcase their knowledge.

Science and Innovation Fair

Our budding innovators showcase their projects and discoveries, inspiring a passion for science and technology among our students.

Annual Function

Every year, the School community comes together to celebrate a night of talent, creativity, and excellence at our much-anticipated Annual Function.

Sports Meet

A showcase of athleticism and sportsmanship, where our student-athletes compete in various sports, promoting teamwork and physical fitness
