Welcome To Woodbine Modern School, Darbhanga

Education | Innovation | Leadership

Established in 1991, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating educational environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. Our mission is to empower the leaders, innovators, and thinkers of tomorrow, and we achieve this through a holistic approach to education that combines rigorous academics with character development and a commitment to community engagement.
Our vision is to be a beacon of excellence in education, inspiring a lifelong love of learning and fostering the growth of well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of an ever-changing world. We aim to create an inclusive and diverse community where every student's unique talents and abilities are recognized and celebrated.

Mrs Nasreen Nawab

-Principal

Principal's Message

We believe that education is a journey, and each day brings new opportunities for growth, discovery, and achievement. Our commitment is to provide an enriching and nurturing environment where students can flourish academically, socially, and personally.

Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about fostering a love of learning in every student. We strive to empower our students with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in an ever-changing world. Beyond academics, we place a strong emphasis on character development, ethical values, and responsible citizenship. We aim to prepare our students not only for success in their chosen paths but also to be compassionate and empathetic individuals who make a positive impact on society.

This is not just a place of education; it is a vibrant community. We encourage our students to explore their interests and talents through a diverse range of extracurricular activities, whether it be in sports, the arts, or community service. These experiences help shape well-rounded individuals and create lasting memories and friendships.

As a parent or guardian, you play an essential role in your child's educational journey. We value your partnership and look forward to working closely with you to support your child's growth and development. Together, we can ensure that each student reaches their full potential.
